## How to Request Deletion
To request deletion of your data, please email us with:
1. Subject: `Data Deletion Request`
2. Your account identifier (username/user ID)
3. Any relevant details to identify your records

## What Will Be Deleted
Upon verification, we will delete or anonymize data associated with your account, including:
- Stored profile/account identifiers
- Generated/scheduled post records
- Performance logs linked to your account

## Timeline
- Acknowledgement: within 7 days
- Deletion completion: typically within 30 days (unless legal retention is required)

## Exceptions
Certain records may be retained if required by law, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

## Confirmation
We will notify you by email once deletion is completed.
